# Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 22, 2026

Side Quest Slayer is a local-first progress journal that helps you track personal quests with optional proof photos, captions, and daily reminder notifications.

## Information the App Accesses

Side Quest Slayer may ask for access to:

- Camera, so you can capture proof photos for your quest entries.
- Photo library, so you can import an existing photo as a quest entry.
- Notifications, so the app can schedule a local daily reminder.

## Information Stored on Your Device

Side Quest Slayer stores your quest titles, optional captions/reflections, photo file locations, and reminder-related app data locally on your device. Photos captured or imported through the app are copied into the app's local document storage.

## No Account, Analytics, Ads, or Cloud Sync

Side Quest Slayer V1 does not require an account and does not send your quest data, captions, or photos to a backend server, analytics service, advertising network, or cloud sync service.

## Sharing

Side Quest Slayer does not sell your data or share your data with third parties. Data may leave your device only if you choose to share it outside the app, back up your device, or use operating-system services that you control.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your quest data remains on your device until you delete it, uninstall the app, or clear the app's storage through your device settings. When you delete entries in the app, Side Quest Slayer attempts to remove the associated locally stored photo.

## Security

Side Quest Slayer limits storage to local app storage and does not maintain a remote copy of your data. Please use your device's lock screen and operating-system security features to help protect local data on your device.

## Children's Privacy

Side Quest Slayer is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through use of the app, contact us so we can help address the concern.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.
